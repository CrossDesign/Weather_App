URL Format and Example

All station data:

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/station_placeholder

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10

Yearly station data:

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/yearly/v1/station_placeholder/date_placeholder

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/yearly/10/1988

Day specific station data:

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/station_placeholder/date_placeholder

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10/19881025

or

http://127.0.0.1:5000/api/v1/10/1988-10-25
